1 Story

Jane has been ten years in a prison
of a cruel stoneman. One day stoneman says
to free Jane if she collects all the lifecrystals
on that planet...

3 Playing
You'll be able to control Jane with <- and -> arrowkeys
and Jane jumps if you press CONTROL. Jane can also go
back to the beginning of a level by pressing ENTER.
PRESS P ANYTIME TO PAUSE GAME. Your object is to get the
lifecrystals on 15 levels.
